## Photography Specifications

### **Camera Settings (for Photographer)**

- **Aperture:** f/1.8 - f/2.8 (shallow depth of field, subject separation)
- **Shutter Speed:** 1/125s minimum (no motion blur)
- **ISO:** 100-400 (low noise, high quality)
- **Focal Length:** 50mm, 85mm, or 135mm (portrait lenses)
- **White Balance:** Custom or 5500K (natural daylight)

### **Lighting Setup**

- **Primary Light:** Large softbox or beauty dish (soft, flattering)
- **Fill Light:** Reflector or low-power fill (reduce shadows)
- **Rim Light:** Optional backlight for separation from background
- **Background Light:** Optional gradient or edge lighting

### **Color Grading / Post-Production**

- **Style:** Cinematic, slightly desaturated but not black-and-white
- **Skin Tone:** Natural (avoid over-smoothing or heavy retouching)
- **Shadows:** Lifted slightly (not crushed blacks)
- **Highlights:** Controlled (no blown-out whites)
- **Color Temperature:** Slightly cool (matches SkaiScraper brand navy/cyan)
- **Sharpness:** High sharpness on eyes, softer on background
