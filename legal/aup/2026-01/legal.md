# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**ClearSkai Technologies, LLC (DBA "SkaiScraper")**  
Prescott, Arizona  
Version: 2026-01  
Effective: January 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs all access to and use of the SkaiScraper platform ("Service").

## 1. Prohibited Conduct

You may NOT use SkaiScraper for any of the following activities:

### 1.1 Fraud, Insurance Misrepresentation, or Deception

You may NOT:

- Use AI outputs to create false claims
- Misrepresent property conditions
- Fabricate hail or wind damage
- Alter metadata to suggest false timelines
- Upload manipulated images to deceive insurers
- Submit AI-generated text as certified inspection results

### 1.2 AI Misuse, Abuse, or Unsafe Operation

You may NOT:

- Rely on AI outputs as authoritative fact
- Use AI to replace licensed professionals
- Generate "evidence" for claims using AI enhancements
- Attempt to force the AI model to produce prohibited content

### 1.3 Illegal, Harmful, or Dangerous Activity

You may NOT:

- Upload illegal content
- Upload copyrighted material without authorization
- Attempt to access unauthorized systems
- Use SkaiScraper for harassment, threats, or discrimination

## 2. Responsibilities of Users

You agree to:

1. Use SkaiScraper responsibly
2. Verify the accuracy of AI outputs
3. Follow all relevant laws and regulations
4. Obtain consent before entering homeowner or client information

## 3. Violations & Enforcement

ClearSkai Technologies, LLC may:

- Suspend or terminate accounts
- Remove content
- Report illegal behavior to authorities
