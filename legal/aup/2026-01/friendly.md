# Acceptable Use Policy — Simple Version

This is the easy-to-understand explanation of the rules.

## What you can NOT do:

### No fraud

- Don't use AI to make fake claims
- Don't exaggerate damage
- Don't mislead insurers or homeowners
- Don't upload fake or altered photos

### No misuse of AI

- AI is not perfect — double-check everything
- Don't use AI to replace engineers, adjusters, or inspectors
- Don't try to force AI to create fake evidence

### No illegal behavior

- No hacking
- No malware
- No harassment
- No stolen copyrighted content

## Your responsibilities

- Use common sense
- Tell the truth
- Be respectful
- Follow the law
- Verify AI output

## If you break the rules

We can suspend your account, remove access, notify insurers, or contact authorities if needed.
