# Service Level Agreement — Simple Version

## Uptime Goal

We try to keep the system available **99.5% of the time** each month.

## When downtime does NOT count:

- Server maintenance
- Your internet connection failing
- Outages from vendors

## AI features:

AI availability is NOT guaranteed.

## Support response time:

- Critical issues: ~2–6 hours
- Major issues: ~12–24 hours

## Refunds:

The SLA does NOT provide refunds or credits.
