## SECTION 1: PLATFORM OVERVIEW

### 1.1 What is SkaiScraper?

SkaiScraper is a cloud-based software platform that connects property owners with verified trades professionals for property damage assessment, repair coordination, and insurance claim support.

### 1.2 Services We Provide to Clients

As a Client, you have access to:

- **Client Portal**: A secure dashboard to manage your property projects and claims
- **Find-a-Pro Directory**: Browse and connect with verified contractors
- **Project Management**: Track project status, communications, and documents
- **Community Network**: Connect with other homeowners, share experiences, and read reviews
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Damage assessments, photo analysis, and documentation assistance
- **Secure Messaging**: Communicate directly with contractors and project teams
- **Document Storage**: Securely store photos, estimates, and project documentation

### 1.3 What We Do NOT Provide

SkaiScraper is a technology platform. **We are NOT:**

- A construction company or contractor
- An insurance company or adjuster
- A legal, engineering, or architectural firm
- A party to contracts between you and contractors
- Responsible for contractor work quality, pricing, or performance
