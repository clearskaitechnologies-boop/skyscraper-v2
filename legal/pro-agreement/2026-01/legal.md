## SECTION 1: PLATFORM OVERVIEW

### 1.1 What is SkaiScraper?

SkaiScraper is a cloud-based software platform designed to help trades professionals manage their business operations, connect with property owners, and streamline insurance restoration work.

### 1.2 Services We Provide to Trades Professionals

As a Trades Professional, you have access to:

- **Pro Dashboard**: Manage your company profile, employees, and operations
- **Job Board**: View and bid on available projects in your area
- **Claims Workspace**: AI-powered tools for damage documentation and estimates
- **Trades Network**: Connect with other professionals and grow your network
- **Client Management**: Track leads, projects, and customer communications
- **AI-Powered Tools**: Damage detection, report generation, and documentation
- **Portfolio Showcase**: Display your work, certifications, and reviews
- **Messaging System**: Communicate with clients and other professionals

### 1.3 What We Do NOT Provide

SkaiScraper is a technology platform. **We are NOT:**

- A general contractor or construction company
- An insurance company, adjuster, or public adjuster
- An engineering, architectural, or legal firm
- An employer—you are an independent business
- Guarantors of work, payment, or client relationships
- A party to contracts between you and clients
